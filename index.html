About.

Jacob Cavin is a content creator who desires to share the Word of God with the world. He founded The Glorify Initiative which is a website that shares Christian content. He also has a history of iOS application development, as well as years of being a YouTube influencer before his conversion. Now, he is attending Bible college in hopes of going into the ministry.

The Glorify Initiative.
